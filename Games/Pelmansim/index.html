    How to Play

    1. Teams roll dice to determine turn order.
    2. On your turn, select one card from the left side (words) and one from the right side (images).
    3. Hover over a word card to see its definition!
    4. Both cards will remain visible for 3 seconds after the second card is revealed.
    5. If you find a matching pair, the cards disappear and you score 1 point. You get another turn immediately.
    6. If the cards don't match, your turn ends and play passes to the next team.
    7. The game ends when all pairs are found. The team with the most points wins!
