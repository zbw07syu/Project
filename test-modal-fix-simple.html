🐺 RunRunRabbit AI Modal Fix Test

Testing Instructions

Step 1: Open the browser's Developer Console (F12 → Console tab)
Step 2: Click the "Open Game" button below to launch RunRunRabbit
Step 3: Set up a game with AI Wolf:
  • Choose any number of teams (2, 3, or 4)
  • Set Wolf as AI (not human)
  • Set at least one rabbit as human
  • Start the game
Step 4: Play until you reach a Paper-Scissors-Stone round where:
  • A human rabbit loses and gets a multiple choice question
  • The question has long options that trigger a modal
  • After answering, the wolf should be next to roll dice
Step 5: Watch the console for debug messages starting with:
  • 🔔 (Modal system messages)
  • 🤖 (AI action messages)
  • 📝 (Question handling messages)
🔍 What to Look For:
After the modal closes, you should see messages like:
• "🔔 Modal system: modalClosed event dispatched"
• "🤖 Modal closed event received"
• "🤖 Processing pending AI player: wolf"
• "🤖 Modal closed - proceeding with AI roll for: wolf"

✅ Success: The AI wolf automatically rolls dice and moves
❌ Failure: The AI wolf gets stuck and doesn't roll
